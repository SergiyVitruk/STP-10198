About the Game

A Fashion Puzzle for the Romantic Soul

Tile Fashion: Love Story is not just a puzzle game — it's a soft and immersive experience built around love, expression, and style.

Designed for those who enjoy relaxing gameplay and beautiful design, this game introduces you to a world of romantic tile sets, storytelling progressions, and outfit reveals.

Match three tiles to clear the board, unlock memories, and piece together stunning fashion looks. Every level is a step deeper into your fashion romance journey — whether you're lounging on a Parisian terrace or wandering through a garden soirée.

How to play?

Chic and Simple Gameplay

  1. Tap to select a tile
  2. Match 3 of the same fashion item to clear.
  3. Complete the level to unveil part of the outfit or story.
  4. Use boosters to gracefully solve trickier sets.
  5. Take your time—this love story unfolds at your own pace.

Game features

  1. 500+ handcrafted levels with romantic visual themes
  2. No timers or lives — a pure, relaxed pace
  3. Fashion-inspired tilesets (dresses, heels, jewelry)
  4. Light narrative progression with chapter unlocks
  5. Zen Mode for infinite matching without goals
  6. Designed for both short breaks and cozy sessions
  7. Offline-ready — your love story goes wherever you go

Outfit progression

Unlock Looks as You Play

Unlock Looks as You Play
 As you complete levels, you collect pieces of fashionable outfits. Each set is tied to a chapter in your character’s love story. From casual coffee-date looks to runway-inspired evening wear, each chapter comes with its own aesthetic — and each match you make brings you closer to completing the ensemble.
