About the Game

A Fashion Puzzle for the Romantic Soul

Tile Fashion: Love Story is not just a puzzle game — it's a soft and immersive experience built around love, expression, and style.

Designed for those who enjoy relaxing gameplay and beautiful design, this game introduces you to a world of romantic tile sets, storytelling progressions, and outfit reveals.

Match three tiles to clear the board, unlock memories, and piece together stunning fashion looks. Every level is a step deeper into your fashion romance journey — whether you're lounging on a Parisian terrace or wandering through a garden soirée.
